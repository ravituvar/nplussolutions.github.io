Easy Gandhinagar

Easy Gandhinagar is an application which facilitates travelers or citizen to find route from their current location to selected destination ,

whether it is home address(i.e. Sector 4 ,Sub Sector A , Plot no:- 1046/1 ) or any public facility like Garden, ATM , Petrol pump , Institute , , Government Offices etc.


It is useful to people who come in Gandhinagar to look for Particular Government Offices.


In further, Easy Gandhinagar assist tourists to find attractions in city like Akshardham Temple ,Mahatma Mandir, Indroda nature park and Sarita Udyan etc. .


This Application also guide to locate particular circle from current location like K5 - Kranti ,K6 - Sanskruti, and G1 - Sangit
